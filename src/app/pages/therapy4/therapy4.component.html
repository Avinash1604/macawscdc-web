Speech Therapy

Helping your child build communication, confidence, and independence through personalized, evidence-based speech therapy.

What is Speech Therapy (OT)?

Speech and Language Therapy supports children in improving their ability to communicate—covering speech clarity, language understanding, expression, and social interaction. At MacawsCDC, we create personalized therapy programs based on each child’s needs using evidence-based approaches.

Our Approach

  • Child-centered and play-based speech therapy
  • Evidence-based communication techniques
  • Individualized therapy plans
  • Family-focused guidance and support

Who Can Benefit?

  • Speech and language delay
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • ADHD (attention & communication challenges)
  • Childhood Apraxia of speech
  • Stuttering / fluency disorders
  • Voice and articulation disorders

Assessment

  • Speech and sound (articulation) evaluation
  • Language comprehension and expression assessment
  • Oral-motor and feeding assessment
  • Fluency and voice analysis
  • Communication and social interaction skills evaluation

Therapy Plan: How It Works

Each child receives an individualized speech therapy plan based on their communication needs.

Key Components

  • Goal setting
  • Speech sound (articulation) training
  • Language development (understanding & expression)
  • Oral-motor and breath control exercises
  • Social communication and interaction skills

Session Structure

Typically 45 minutes, based on the child’s individual needs

Regular therapy sessions (as per plan)

Continuous monitoring with progress reviews and updates

Parent Involvement

Home-based speech practice activities

Guidance for improving communication in daily routines

Regular feedback and progress updates

Benefits of Speech Therapy

  • Improved speech clarity and pronunciation
  • Better language understanding and expression
  • Enhanced communication and social interaction
  • Improved listening and attention skills
  • Increased confidence in speaking
  • Better school readiness and academic performance
