Behaviour Therapy

Helping children build positive behaviors, communication skills, and independence through structured, evidence-based therapy

What is Behavior Therapy?

Behavior Therapy is an evidence-based approach that helps children build positive behaviors, communication skills, and independence by understanding triggers and guiding appropriate responses. It is commonly used for children with autism, ADHD, developmental delays, and communication challenges.

Our Approach

  • Child-centered and play-based therapy
  • Evidence-based techniques
  • Individualized care plans
  • Family-focused support

Who Can Benefit?

Behavior Therapy supports children with:
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • ADHD
  • Speech and language delays
  • Global developmental delays
  • Behavioral and emotional regulation challenges

Assessment: Understanding Your Child’s Needs

A personalized therapy plan begins with:
  • Parent discussions and developmental history
  • Observation of behavior and triggers
  • Identification of strengths and challenges

Therapy Plan: How It Works

Each child receives an individualized plan based on their needs.

Key Components

  • Goal setting
  • Individualized Therapy
  • Parent Involvement
  • Progress Tracking

Session Structure

  • Typically 30–45 minutes based on individual needs
  • Regular therapy schedule (based on availability)
  • Continuous progress reviews and plan updates

Techniques We Use

  • Positive reinforcement (rewards and motivation)
  • Prompting and gradual fading
  • Repetition and consistency
  • Visual supports and cues
  • Real-life scenario practice

Benefits of Behavior Therapy

  • Improves communication and language skills
  • Reduces challenging behaviors
  • Enhances social interaction and play
  • Builds independence in daily activities
  • Improves attention and learning ability
  • Boosts confidence and emotional regulation

Why Early Intervention Matters

Starting early helps:

  • Build strong foundational skills
  • Reduce long-term behavioral challenges
  • Improve independence and quality of life
