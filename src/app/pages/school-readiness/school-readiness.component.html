School Readiness Program

Preparing children for a confident and successful start to school.

What is School Readiness Program?

Our School Readiness Program is designed to help children develop the essential skills needed to transition smoothly into a structured classroom environment. We focus on building independence, communication, attention, and early academic foundations.

Our Approach

  • Child-centered and play-based therapy
  • Evidence-based techniques
  • Individualized care plans
  • Family-focused support
  • Structured classroom readiness activitiest

Who Can Benefit?

  • Children with delayed school readiness skills
  • Difficulty following classroom routines and instructions
  • Attention and concentration challenges
  • Speech and communication delays
  • Social interaction and peer engagement difficulties
  • Fine motor and pre-writing skill delays
  • Behavioral and emotional regulation concerns

Assessment

  • Developmental and learning readiness evaluation
  • Attention, listening, and classroom participation assessment
  • Communication and social interaction screening
  • Fine motor and pre-writing skill assessment
  • Cognitive and pre-academic skill evaluation
  • Behavioral and sensory observation
  • Functional independence and adaptive skills review

Therapy Plan: How It Works

Each child receives an individualized school readiness program designed according to their developmental level, learning style, and specific needs.

Key Components

  • Attention and listening skill development
  • Classroom routine and instruction-following practice
  • Communication and social interaction training
  • Fine motor and pre-writing skill activities
  • Early learning and cognitive skill development
  • Behavioral and emotional regulation support
  • Independence and self-help skill training
  • Parent guidance and home-based activity support

Session Structure

Typically 45–60 minutes based on the child’s developmental needs, attention span, and learning goals.

Parent Involvement

Includes home-based learning activities, classroom routine guidance, progress updates, and regular parent feedback sessions to support consistency between therapy, home, and school environments.

Benefits of School Readiness Program

  • Improved attention and classroom participation
  • Better communication and social interaction
  • Enhanced pre-academic and learning skills
  • Improved fine motor and writing readiness
  • Increased independence in classroom routines
  • Better emotional and behavioral regulation
  • Greater confidence in school environments

Why Early Intervention Matters

Early support helps children develop essential foundational skills before entering formal schooling. Timely intervention can improve learning ability, social adaptation, communication, and overall school success, leading to smoother transitions and better long-term outcomes.
