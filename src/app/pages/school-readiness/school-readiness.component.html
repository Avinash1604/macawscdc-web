School Readiness Program

Preparing children for a confident start to school

Overview

Our School Readiness Program helps children develop early learning, social, and self-care skills needed for kindergarten and primary school.

Core Areas

  • Fine motor and pre-writing skills
  • Language and early literacy
  • Social skills and classroom routines
  • Self-help and independence

Program Structure

Weekly sessions include play-based learning, small-group activities, and parent guidance to reinforce skills at home.

Who Should Join

Children approaching school age or those who would benefit from targeted readiness support.
